My Portfolio

Things I've worked on recently

Mealtime

Roles: Product Owner, Lead Frontend Developer

Mealtime is a business focused product that generates meal packs for local grocery stores. Think HelloFresh, but at the local store level. As the product owner and lead frontend developer, I helped align the development team towards the product goals and created the design for the frontend of the app.

Saga

Roles: Tech Lead, Full-Stack Developer

Saga is an app that aggregates movie and tv show data. As the tech lead for this product, I delegated tasks related to all technical aspects of Saga and oversaw the development process of the app.

GGs

Solo Project

GG's is a full stack solo project I developed to help log user data related to their favorite video games. Users can search for games and leave personal ratings and comments on the games they love (and hate).

MTGTranslate

Solo Project

This is one of my first solo projects. MTGTranslate takes English Magic: The Gathering cards and translates them to Japanese. This project is unique as it uses Python and Django for the backend with minimal frontend.
